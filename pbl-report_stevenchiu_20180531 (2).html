Definition of subfertility and infertility:

Terminlogy in reproductive medicince is confusing and misleading. It is better to inroduce the definition of subfertility which is more general than infertility[1].

  1. Subfertility: Describes any form of reduced fertility with prolonged time of unwanted non-conception (不受孕).
  2. Infertility: Defined as 1 year of unwanted non-conception with unprotected intercourse in the feritile phase of the menstrual cycles

Why is the definition subfertility important?

It is gerneral problem of epidemiological research in th definition of subfertility and its prevalence in gereral population interact.

Therefore, time-to-pregnant estimation [TTP, cumiulative probabilities of conception(CPC)] are the fundamental importance to find suitable thresholds to determine the prevalence of grades of subfertility.

The tresholds is the major indicator to be used to indentify subfertility.

  1. Premature intervention: As incraseasingly couples soon seek advice about infertility care with danger of false-positive results and following unnecesary over-treatment.
  2. Late intevection: may represent infertility under-treatment.
